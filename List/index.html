Film Making Terms

Arc Shot
Interlude
Prolouge

Arc shot

A shot in which the subject is photographed by an encircling or moving camera

Interlude

A brief intervening film scene or sequence not specially tied to the plot that appears within a film

Prolouge

A speech preface introduction or brief scene preceding to the main action or plot of a film contrast to epilogue
